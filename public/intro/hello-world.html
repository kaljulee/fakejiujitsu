Introduction Hello World

Hello, and thank you for your interest in Fake Jiu Jitsu! Sometime in 2020 I stopped trying to actually learn Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, and started just faking it.

This document is my attempt to express my ideas clearly, both for my own growth and to help any other grapplers asking the forbidden questions of BJJ.

There are 6 sections, each divided into subsections:

  1. Introduction
  2. Fake Jiu Jitsu 2.0t LTS
  3. Justifications
  4. FJJ Technique Applications
  5. Deprecated FJJ Versions
  6. Appendix

My goal is to have each of these sections independently readable, so you can skip to whatever section is most interesting.
