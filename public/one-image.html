Introduction Fake Jiu Jitsu in One Image

Many people have created excellent mind maps of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. But even the best of these are either highly incomplete or highly complex (or both).

I believe the reason BJJ is so hard to map is because it uses a sub-optimal set of fundamental reference points.

Like a geocentric mapping of planetary movement, a Helio-centric mapping of BJJ results in a usable but unnecessarily complex system.

Fake Jiu Jitsu proposes a different set of fundamentals, resulting in a much simpler map.
