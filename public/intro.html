Quick Start Introduction

Hello, and thank you for your interest in Fake Jiu Jitsu! Sometime in 2020 I stopped trying to actually learn Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, and started just faking it. It’s been much more fun than learning how to do the real thing, and I would argue it has not significantly hindered my development as a grappler.

This document is my attempt to express my ideas clearly, both for my own growth and to help any other grapplers asking the forbidden questions of BJJ.
